The purpose for this page is to share with our community the list of services and service provider categories we need in our region as determined by our Resource Development department. This team identifies these needs through consultation from our case management staff, clinical staff,  and various community groups and forums. If you are a professional qualified to provide any of the following services please consider pursuing our RFP process. If you know a provider who might be interested please forward the RFP to them for their consideration.

Status: Open

Deadline for Submission: Until filled

Valley Mountain Regional Center has the immediate need for Clinical Psychologists with experience in reviewing psychological assessments and cognitive testing reports and providing recommendations if an individual meets the California Lanterman Act Eligibility Requirements. The psychologist will provide a comprehensive record review and will confirm if the individual's disability originated prior to age 18, is expected to be life long and constitutes as substantial disability for the individual. In addition, the psychologist will provide recommendations if the individual has a qualifying condition -Intellectual Disability, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Cerebral Palsy, Epilepsy, or other developmental disability (5th category). Along with providing confirmation of the qualifying condition, the psychologist will determine if the individual meets three or more of the Substantial Disability qualification with deficits in self-care, communication, learning, mobility, self-direction, capacity of independent living, and economic self-sufficiency. VMRC will provide the psychologist with an overview training of eligibility requirements and Lanterman services.
